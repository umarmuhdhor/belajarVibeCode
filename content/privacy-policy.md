**Last updated: June 2026**

PopShot ("we", "us", or "our") is a mobile application that lets users participate in gamified photo challenges during group trips. This policy describes how we handle information when you use PopShot.

## What We Collect

**Account information.** When you create an account, we collect your display name and a profile photo if you choose to add one.

**Trip data.** We store trip names, challenge types, and the photos you submit during a trip. Photos are shared with other members of the same trip.

**Device information.** We may collect basic device information such as operating system version to diagnose app errors.

We do not collect your precise location. We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not run advertising in the app.

## How We Use Your Data

- To create and manage trips and challenges within the app.
- To display photos to other members of your trip.
- To build the shared album when a trip ends.
- To diagnose bugs and improve app stability.

## Photo Sharing

Photos you take during a trip are visible to all members of that trip. After a trip ends, photos appear in the shared album. You should only participate in trips with people you trust.

## Data Retention

Trip data and photos are retained while your account is active. You can contact us to request deletion of your account and associated data.

## Children

PopShot is not intended for children under 13. If you are under 13, please do not use the app.

## Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. We will notify users of significant changes within the app.
